A basic creamy tomato soup but with cottage cheese.

### Dietary Information

- **Allergens:** Milk.

### Ingredients

- <RecipeUnit met={[400, "ml"]} us={[13.5, "fl.oz"]} client:idle /> hot water
- <RecipeUnit met={[800, "g"]} us={[14, "oz"]} client:idle /> canned peeled
  tomato
- <RecipeUnit met={[0.5, ""]} us={[0.5, ""]} client:idle /> onion, chopped
- Unsalted butter
- Salt
- Black pepper
- <RecipeUnit met={[2, "tbsp"]} us={[2, "tbsp"]} client:idle /> cottage cheese
  (and extra for serving)

And some of your favourite bread, toasted with butter.

### Directions

1. Melt butter and add the onion in a small pot
2. Add in the peeled tomatos and bring to a boil.
3. Mix in the hot water.
4. Season with salt and black pepper to your liking.
5. Let the soup cool down for about 10 minutes.
6. Carefully place the soup in a blender, and blend.
7. Add <RecipeUnit met={[2, " tablespoons"]} us={[2, " tablespoons"]} client:idle />
   of cottage cheese, and let it blend for a minute or two.

Served with extra cottage cheese in the bowl.
